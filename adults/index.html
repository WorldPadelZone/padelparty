Love at First Serve 💕

Our signature Padel Americano Speed Dating events are taking Cape Town by storm! Meet 8-12 potential matches in one fun-filled evening.

💘

How It Works

Arrive for welcome drinks, then rotate through 7-minute mini-padel matches. Each "date" is a fun doubles game where conversation flows naturally!

⚡

No Pressure Zone

The focus is on having fun and playing padel! This takes the awkwardness out of traditional speed dating and creates genuine connections.

🎯

Smart Matching

After the event, we'll send you contact details for mutual matches. Plus, everyone gets invited to our WhatsApp community!

🌟

Age-Specific Events

We host separate events for different age groups (25-35, 35-45, 45+) to ensure compatibility and comfort for everyone.
